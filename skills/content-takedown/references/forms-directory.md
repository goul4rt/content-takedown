## Search / privacy
- Google private info / doxxing: support.google.com → "Remove my private info from Google Search"
- Google "Results about you": myactivity.google.com/results-about-you (monitors and removes PII from Search, not from the source)
- Google copyright removal (Search/DMCA) and Legal Help removal tools
- Bing content removal / "content removal request" tools

## NCII / CSAM
- StopNCII.org (adults; on-device PDQ/PhotoDNA hash for images, MD5 for videos; accepts AI deepfakes of self)
- NCMEC Take It Down: takeitdown.ncmec.org (subjects under 18 in the image)
- NCMEC CyberTipline: report.cybertip.org / cybertipline.org
- FTC TAKE IT DOWN non-compliance reports: TakeItDown.ftc.gov
- INHOPE (hotline network); IWF (report and hash list); PHAROS (internet-signalement.gouv.fr); SaferNet BR (new.safernet.org.br)

## Brand / IP programs (trademark, NOT DMCA)
- Amazon Brand Registry; eBay VeRO; Meta Brand Rights Protection; Google trademark complaint; TikTok IP reporting; Shopify IP/DMCA

## Domain disputes
- WIPO UDRP (transfer remedy; standard about US$1,500 for a single-panel case of 1 to 5 domains; about a 2-month timeline; expedited option about US$4,000 for a 1-month decision; verify)
- URS providers (suspension only): FORUM, ADNDRC, MFSD (about US$225 to 375 for up to 5 domains; verify)
